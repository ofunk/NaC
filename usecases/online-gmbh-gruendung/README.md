The source repository was empty. This folder is the canonical NoC location for
the online GmbH-/UG-Gruendung usecase.

## Goal

Prepare a notary-office usecase package for GmbH or UG formation. The package
binds founder intake, company data, articles or model protocol, managing
director appointment, shareholder list, card/XNP readiness and commercial
register filing evidence.

## Scope

- Intake for company name, legal form, seat, business address, object, founders,
  capital, shares and management.
- Identity, representation, beneficial-owner and AML review flags.
- Online or in-office route selection with card, XNP and register readiness.
- Draft and filing package preparation with human review gates.

## Out of Scope

- No final legal advice generated by the LLM.
- No automatic register filing without reviewed XNP/Handelsregister connector.
- No real founder, address, share or AML data in Git.

- Model protocol or individual articles.
- GmbH or UG and cash or in-kind contribution route.
- Online, in-office or hybrid notarization route.
- Whether AML or beneficial-owner review blocks the next step.
